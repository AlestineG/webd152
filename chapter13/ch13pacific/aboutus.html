About Us

Hey there! I'm Melanie Bowie, the proud owner of Pacific Trails Resort. I love nature and I am excited to create unforgettable experiences for nature-loving couples and groups of people. At Pacific Trails Resort, we want to give locals a chance to escape the hustle and bustle of city life for a weekend and offer travelers unique experiences. Our resort has awesome daytime activities like kayaking and hiking, and at night, you can enjoy a peaceful and luxurious dining experience or even sleep under the stars. I'm dedicated to providing a one-of-a-kind and memorable getaway for those who appreciate nature and love the outdoors. I can't wait to welcome you to Pacific Trails Resort and help you make some amazing memories!
